Functional Expense Allocation

GAAP REQUIREMENT (ASC 958-720)
Voluntary health and welfare organizations must present a Statement of Functional Expenses. Other nonprofits are encouraged but not required. Form 990 Part IX requires all filers to report expenses by function.

THREE FUNCTIONAL CATEGORIES:
1. Program Services — activities that fulfill the organization's mission
   - For RI: housing operations, property maintenance, tenant services, construction
2. Management & General (M&G) — overall organizational management, finance, admin
   - Includes: accounting, legal, board activities, insurance, general office
3. Fundraising — activities to generate contributions
   - Includes: solicitation costs, donor cultivation, fundraising events

ALLOCATION METHODS
Expenses that serve a single function are directly assigned. Expenses that serve multiple functions must be allocated using a reasonable methodology:

1. Direct identification: expense clearly serves one function
2. Time-and-effort studies: allocate based on staff time logs
3. Square footage: allocate occupancy costs by program space usage
4. Headcount: allocate shared costs by number of staff per function
5. Salary allocation: allocate benefits and payroll taxes based on salary allocation

FORM 990 PART IX
Reports expenses by natural classification (rows) and functional allocation (columns):
- Column A: Total
- Column B: Program services
- Column C: Management & general
- Column D: Fundraising

Must describe method used in Schedule O if not obvious.

JOINT COST ALLOCATION (ASC 958-720-45)
Activities that include both program and fundraising content (e.g., direct mail with educational content and a solicitation) may allocate costs between program and fundraising IF all three criteria are met:
1. Purpose criterion: would the activity have been conducted even without the fundraising component?
2. Audience criterion: is the audience selected based on program need (not donor capacity)?
3. Content criterion: does the activity include program content that would accomplish the mission?

If all three criteria NOT met, all costs are charged to fundraising.

RI-SPECIFIC CONTEXT
RI's primary program is affordable housing. Most expenses (property operations, maintenance, utilities, depreciation) are directly assignable to program services. Salary and office costs require allocation between program and M&G based on time studies or reasonable estimates.
