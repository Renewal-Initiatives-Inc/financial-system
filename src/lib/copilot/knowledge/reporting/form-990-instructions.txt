Source: IRS Form 990 Instructions (2024); Form 990 Schedule A, D, L

Form 990 — Return of Organization Exempt From Income Tax

FILING THRESHOLDS
- Form 990: gross receipts >= $200,000 OR total assets >= $500,000
- Form 990-EZ: gross receipts < $200,000 AND total assets < $500,000
- Form 990-N (e-postcard): gross receipts normally <= $50,000

PART IX — STATEMENT OF FUNCTIONAL EXPENSES
Key line items for RI:
Line 1: Grants and similar amounts paid
Line 5: Compensation of current officers, directors
Line 7: Other salaries and wages
Line 8: Pension/retirement plan contributions
Line 9: Other employee benefits
Line 10: Payroll taxes
Line 11: Fees for services (legal, accounting, lobbying, investment management, other)
Line 13: Office expenses
Line 14: Information technology
Line 15: Royalties
Line 16: Occupancy
Line 17: Travel
Line 18: Payments of travel for officials
Line 19: Conferences, conventions, meetings
Line 20: Interest
Line 21: Payments to affiliates
Line 22: Depreciation, depletion, amortization
Line 23: Insurance
Line 24: Other expenses (up to 6 categories)
Line 25: Total functional expenses
Columns: (A) Total, (B) Program services, (C) Management & general, (D) Fundraising

PART X — BALANCE SHEET
Line 1-16: Assets
Line 17-25: Liabilities
Line 27: Net assets without donor restrictions
Line 28: Net assets with donor restrictions
Line 29: Total net assets
Line 33: Total liabilities and net assets

SCHEDULES
Schedule A: Public Charity Status and Public Support (required for all 501(c)(3))
Schedule B: Schedule of Contributors (confidential, not public)
Schedule D: Supplemental Financial Statements
Schedule L: Transactions with Interested Persons
Schedule O: Supplemental Information (narrative explanations)

RI-SPECIFIC CONTEXT
RI currently qualifies for Form 990-EZ. Post-property development (assets > $500K), RI will file the full Form 990. The system maps account types and sub-types to Form 990 Part IX line numbers via the form990Line field on the accounts table.
