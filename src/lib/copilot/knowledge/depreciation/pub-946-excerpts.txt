COMPONENT ALLOCATION
At PIS date, total cost must be allocated among components:
1. Land (not depreciable — typically 10-20% of total for residential)
2. Building (27.5-year)
3. Land improvements (15-year)
4. Personal property (5 or 7-year)
Cost segregation study may optimize allocation but is less relevant for tax-exempt entities.

RI-SPECIFIC CONTEXT
RI's CIP (Construction in Progress) accumulates costs during development. At PIS date, CIP is converted to fixed asset components using cost code categories (HARD_COST, SOFT_COST) as a starting point for allocation. The system tracks depreciation by component after conversion.
