Net Asset Releases — Reclassification Mechanics

WHEN TO RELEASE
Net assets are released from donor restrictions when the restriction is satisfied:
- Purpose restriction: when the expenditure for the specified purpose is made
- Time restriction: when the specified time period passes
- Long-lived asset restriction: when asset is placed in service (under RI's policy)

JOURNAL ENTRY
Dr: Net Assets Released — With Donor Restrictions (decreases restricted net assets)
Cr: Net Assets Released — Without Donor Restrictions (increases unrestricted net assets)

This entry appears on the Statement of Activities. It does NOT affect total net assets — it is a reclassification between classes.

SIMULTANEOUS RECEIPT AND RELEASE
If a restricted contribution is received and the restriction is met in the same period, the organization has a policy choice:
1. Show in restricted, then release (two entries)
2. Show directly in unrestricted (if policy elected and consistently applied)
RI should use method 1 for clarity in fund tracking.

PARTIAL RELEASES
If a restricted fund is partially spent, release only the amount that satisfies the restriction:
- Example: $500K AHP grant received. $200K spent on construction in FY2025.
- Release $200K from restricted to unrestricted.
- $300K remains in restricted net assets.

MULTI-FUND RESTRICTIONS
Some grants have multiple conditions/restrictions:
- All conditions must be met before recognizing as revenue
- All restrictions must be satisfied before releasing
- Track each separately in the fund

FORM 990 REPORTING
- Statement of Revenue: restricted contributions on Line 1c or 8
- Statement of Functional Expenses: expenses by natural class and function
- Net asset releases: shown as a separate line on Statement of Activities
- Balance Sheet (Part X): Net assets without donor restrictions (Line 27), with donor restrictions (Line 28)

RI-SPECIFIC CONTEXT
RI's primary release event is construction completion/PIS date. When the property is placed in service, restricted construction funds (AHP, CPA, MassDev) are released to unrestricted. The GL engine handles this with automatic release journal entries triggered by the PIS date posting.
