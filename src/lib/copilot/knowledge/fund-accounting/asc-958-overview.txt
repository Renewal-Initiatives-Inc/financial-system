Source: ASC 958 — Not-for-Profit Entities; FASB ASU 2016-14

ASC 958 — Net Asset Classification for Nonprofits

OVERVIEW
ASC 958 (updated by ASU 2016-14, effective for annual periods beginning after December 15, 2017) establishes the framework for nonprofit financial reporting. Key change: replaced three net asset classes (unrestricted, temporarily restricted, permanently restricted) with two:

TWO NET ASSET CLASSES:
1. Without Donor Restrictions — resources available for general operations
   - Includes board-designated funds (internal, not donor-imposed)
   - Includes operating surplus
   - Formerly "unrestricted"

2. With Donor Restrictions — resources subject to donor-imposed restrictions
   - Time restrictions: must be used in a future period
   - Purpose restrictions: must be used for a specified purpose
   - Perpetual restrictions: principal must be maintained in perpetuity (endowments)
   - Combines former "temporarily restricted" and "permanently restricted"

NET ASSET RELEASES (ASC 958-205-45-6)
When a purpose or time restriction is satisfied, a reclassification is made:
- Debit: Net Assets With Donor Restrictions
- Credit: Net Assets Without Donor Restrictions
This appears on the Statement of Activities as "Net assets released from restrictions."

STATEMENT OF ACTIVITIES FORMAT
Must present:
- Changes in net assets without donor restrictions
- Changes in net assets with donor restrictions
- Total change in net assets
- Beginning net assets (by class)
- Ending net assets (by class)

STATEMENT OF FINANCIAL POSITION
Must present:
- Total assets
- Total liabilities
- Net assets without donor restrictions
- Net assets with donor restrictions
- Total net assets

UNDERWATER ENDOWMENTS (ASC 958-205-45-10)
If fair value of endowment fund falls below original gift amount, the deficiency is reported in net assets with donor restrictions (not without).

RI-SPECIFIC CONTEXT
RI uses two net asset classes in the fund structure:
- General Fund (unrestricted) = Net Assets Without Donor Restrictions
- Restricted funds = Net Assets With Donor Restrictions
The system maps fund restriction type to net asset class for financial statement presentation.
